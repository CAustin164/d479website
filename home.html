Getting to Taniti

Though most arrive by air, many cruise ships dock here in Yellow Leaf Bay. With the expanding of the airport its never been easier to visit this beautiful island. Book your travel here.

Transportation

Public buses and taxis run day and night to get you to your destination. Need something more private? cars are available for rent at a local rental agency. Prefer to explore on foot? Taniti city is very flat and walkable tourist location.
